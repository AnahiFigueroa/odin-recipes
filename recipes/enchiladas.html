How to make Enchiladas!

Ingredients you will need

  • 10 Tortillas
  • Chile Guajillo
  • One garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of Oregano
  • 2 pounds of chicken breast
  • Sour cream
  • Cheese
  • Onion

Instructions

  1. Boil your chiles in hot water until they are ready
  2. In a blender blend your chiles, garlic, salt, oregano
  3. In a pan, add oil of your choosing
  4. While the oil is heating up in the pan, warm up your tortillas for 30-40 seconds on your microwave
  5. Dip the tortillas in the sauce and and then fry them
  6. Add your chicken and roll your enchilada
  7. Repeat Steps
  8. Finish off by adding cheese, sour cream

Enjoy!
